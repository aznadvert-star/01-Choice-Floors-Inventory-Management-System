About this product

The American Harvest Oak series represents the pinnacle of North American hardwood craftsmanship. Harvested from sustainable managed forests, each plank is selected for its natural character and structural integrity. The 3/4" thickness provides a lifetime of durability, allowing for multiple sandings and refinishings over decades of use.

Our matte UV-cured urethane finish offers superior protection against daily wear and tear while maintaining the raw, natural aesthetic of the white oak species. The 5-inch width provides a classic look that complements both traditional and contemporary interiors.
